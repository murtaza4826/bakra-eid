Bakra Eid Mubarak

Bakra Eid, also known as Eid-ul-Adha, is an important Islamic festival celebrated by Muslims around the world. It honors the sacrifice and obedience of Prophet Ibrahim (A.S). People celebrate this special day by offering prayers, sacrificing animals, sharing meat with family and the needy and spreading love and happiness.
